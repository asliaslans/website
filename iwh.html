Institute for Water and Health

Safe water, a sustainable environment, and resilient communities · Georgia Southern University

“Access to safe water is a global challenge. It unlocks economic development, improves health, and provides opportunities.” — Dr. Asli Aslan, IWH Director

The Institute for Water and Health (IWH) bridges science and technology to action. Located at the Savannah Research Complex near the Armstrong Campus, the Institute operates state-of-the-art research laboratories that translate scientific progress into practical technological solutions for water quality, environmental health, and community resilience.

Primary Goals

  • Translate scientific advancements into customized technological tools
  • Support decision-makers implementing policy improvements
  • Develop comprehensive educational resources for water professionals
  • Build a skilled workforce supporting regional growth
